Serve Verdict

Sinner edges the serve battle in efficiency and consistency — higher % 1st serve in, fewer double faults, better break point saving, and a slightly higher serve rating overall. Sinner's serve in 2025 is arguably the best in the world by serve rating, finishing No.1 in service games won percentage at year-end.

Return Verdict

Sinner is the best returner in the world — possibly the greatest single-season returner in ATP history. In 2025, he posted a 56.45% total points won rate, an historic figure. This return dominance is Sinner's most decisive advantage in the rivalry.

Peak Week Too Close to Call
Each player's single most dominant week in tennis history
🇮🇹 AO 2024 — The Comeback
Week of 14 Jan 2024
Down 2 sets to 0 vs Djokovic in SF, then won 3 straight sets. Beat Medvedev in the final from 2 sets down — only the 9th player in Open Era to win a major from that deficit. 5 matches, 4 Top-20 opponents, zero retirements, closed it out 6-3 in the 5th after a 3h45m final.
🇪🇸 USO 2022 — The Coronation
Week of 29 Aug 2022
Beat Sinner (5h15m, 2:50AM finish), Nadal, and Djokovic in the same slam to win his first major aged 19. Three of the greatest players ever, in consecutive rounds. Djokovic was defending champion. Became youngest world No.1 ever a week later. No debut slam run in history compares.
